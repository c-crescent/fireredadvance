  - [ ] Shorter Oak Hall of Fame speech.

### Catch' Em All

- [ ] Removed all friendship evolutions.
  - [ ] All baby Pokemon now evolve at level 12.
  - [ ] Umbreon and Espeon now evolve with Moon Stone & Sun Stone respectively.
- [ ] All Pokemons are now infinitely catchable. 
  - [ ] All missing legendary / mythical Pokemon added as static encounters.
  - [ ] All legendary Pokemon now resets after entering the Hall of Fame.
  - [ ] All non-legendary / mythical pokemon now obtainable in the wild before the E4 without breeding. Rarer species become infinitely recatchable after entering the Hall of Fame.

### Moves

- [ ] Hidden Power BP is now always 70.
- [ ] All attacking moves with less than 100 BP have had their accuracy increased to 100.
- [ ] All status moves except sleep-inducing ones have had their accuracy increased to 100.
- [ ] Flattened the learnsets of all evolution lines so that there's no longer an incentive to delay evolutions. This also means stone evolutions no longer has learnset issues.
- [ ] Doubled Triple Kick damage.
